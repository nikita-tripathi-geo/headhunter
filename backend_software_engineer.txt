About the Role

We’re looking for an exceptional backend software engineer to join the Monetization Team at X, a high-impact group responsible for driving significant revenue through three key products: X Premium, X Verified Organizations, and the Creator Revenue Sharing Program. These products serve millions of users and businesses daily, powering subscriptions, growth, and creator monetization on one of the world’s largest platforms.

As a backend engineer, you’ll design and build scalable, high-performance systems that power subscriptions infrastructure, new monetized products, user growth, and creator monetization. You’ll work on mission-critical infrastructure that processes millions of transactions, optimizes user acquisition and retention, and enables creators to earn a living through their content. This is a unique opportunity to make a large-scale revenue impact while creating products that users and businesses love.

Design and build robust subscriptions infrastructure, handling payments and SKU technology across devices and platforms for millions of daily users.
